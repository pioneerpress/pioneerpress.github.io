Who we are

The Pioneer Press, a Media News Group newspaper and the winner of three Pulitzer Prizes, serves readers and advertisers in the Twin Cities’ East Metro region and Western Wisconsin. The newspaper also is available at more than 1,300 West Metro and Minneapolis locations, including newspaper boxes and retail store newsstands. The Pioneer Press – with its familiar bulldog brand – reflects the excellent journalism, distinctive personalities and tenacious, loyal and dependable spirit readers and advertisers find in Minnesota’s First Newspaper.
